I have started and abandoned more blogs than I care to admit.

Each time, the impulse is the same: _I want to think in public._ And each time, the friction wins. The setup, the theme, the question of what I am even supposed to be saying — it accumulates into a kind of confusion, and the words sits there, dated and unsent.

This time, I am doing it differently. I am writing the first post before I have any audience, any agenda, or any plan. Only the sentences that feel more honest will be accompanied by another sentence.

---

There will be more. I do not know when, or about what exactly. But the archive is open now, and that is enough to start.

_— Laz_
